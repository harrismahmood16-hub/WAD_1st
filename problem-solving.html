Restaurant Digital Transformation — Case Study

Business context & goals

As CTO for a local restaurant, the goal is to support a low-cost digital transformation that improves customer engagement, modernises communication channels, and streamlines bookings without adding significant operational overhead. The solution must be web-based, accessible on mobile devices, integrate social media links, and avoid server-side databases (per assessment brief).

Required features implemented

  • Integration with social media: clear, visible links to Facebook, Instagram and Twitter/X.
  • Display of the restaurant’s contact details (email and phone).
  • Interactive booking/feedback form that builds an email to restaurant staff when submitted (no database).
