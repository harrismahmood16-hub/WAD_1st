A Luxury Dining Experience

Located in the heart of Sheffield, Robertos blends traditional Italian heritage with contemporary fine-dining artistry. Our chefs use the finest ingredients sourced from Italy and local Yorkshire producers to create dishes that honour flavour, craftsmanship, and unforgettable hospitality.

Whether you're enjoying a signature pasta, a premium cut from our grill selection, or a handcrafted dessert, every plate is designed to captivate your senses.
